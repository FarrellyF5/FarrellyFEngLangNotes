Word Classes

These are the types of words used in English

Nouns

Proper nouns describe specific things. They are spelt with capital letters. Proper nouns are also known as names.
Examples include: Marcus, United Kingdom, Mars, Tokyo, and Pepsi

All nouns that aren't proper nouns are known as common nouns, the types of which are:

Concrete nouns refer to things that physically exist, these are things you can sense.
Examples include: chair, computer, bird, building, and apple

Abstract nouns on the other hand are things that do not exist physically, such as feelings, ideas, and qualities.
Examples include: love, marxism, joy, frequency, and time

Collective nouns describe groups of things.
Examples include: flock, herd, class, society, and tribe.
